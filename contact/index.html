Frequently Asked Questions

How can I order books for my school or library? +

We offer special discounts for bulk orders from educational institutions. Please contact us at orders@chalkandwonderpress.com with your requirements, and our educational sales team will assist you with pricing and ordering information.

Do you accept manuscript submissions? +

Yes, we accept unsolicited manuscripts from authors. Please review our submission guidelines on the "Submissions" page before sending your work. Due to the volume of submissions we receive, please allow 8-12 weeks for a response.

Are your authors available for school visits? +

Many of our authors and illustrators are available for school visits, library events, and workshops. Please contact us with details about your event, and we'll connect you with the appropriate creator's booking information.

Where can I purchase your books? +

Our books are available through major online retailers, independent bookstores, and directly from our website. Visit the individual book pages for purchasing options. If you'd like to see our books in your local bookstore, please ask them to order from our distributor.

How can illustrators submit their portfolios? +

We're always looking for talented illustrators! Please email a link to your online portfolio to illustrations@chalkandwonderpress.com. Include examples of your work with children's subjects and any published work in your portfolio.
